About ADS Interconnects

Advanced Digital Systems (ADS) interconnects is incorporated in 2016 with an idea, a desire to create & and achieve to become premier in providing end-to-end design and development solutions to meet Aerospace, Defense, Health care, Networking and Telecom Engineering industries requirements in India. It led by experienced professionals with vast experience in the fields of engineering, integration, manufacturing domain in both public and private sectors.

We do provide support as per customer needs like

- Onsite and offsite Engineering support and service on need basis.
- Support Custom project work tailored to your specific needs.

We are authorized India Channel Partner for The Goebel Company (USA), a supplier of bus interfaces, simulation, flight recorders, Test and analyzer solutions for the avionics industry, developed with industry leading capabilities by focusing on select programs spanning a group of Fortune 50 companies.
